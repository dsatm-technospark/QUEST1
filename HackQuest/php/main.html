HackQuest

Problem Statement

“Developing a Unique and Impactful Solution to a Current Societal Issue”

Objective

To identify, analyse, and develop innovative solutions to pressing societal problems, leveraging technology, creativity, and collaboration.

Context

Society today faces numerous challenges ranging from environmental issues, healthcare crises, educational barriers, and socio-economic disparities. While technological advancements offer potential solutions, there remains a gap between innovative ideas and practical implementations. This hackathon aims to bridge that gap by bringing together diverse minds to brainstorm and develop viable solutions that can make a tangible impact on society.
